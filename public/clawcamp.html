The problem

Mind maps that can't see each other.

Every person, every AI, every team carries a private mental map of shared reality. They can't see each other. Descriptions drift silently from what they describe.

The AI field named this at the agent scale: context rot — agents drown in retrieved text, reasoning degrades. The same failure hollows out teams over a quarter, communities over a generation. Same shape. Different substrate.
