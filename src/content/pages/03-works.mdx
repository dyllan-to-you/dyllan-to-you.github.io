Imagine a personal wikipedia for your life, managed and maintained automatically. Every concept, relationship, habit, goal, and memory — linked, searchable, coherent, and yours. Local-first, so it's yours — not Google's, not some VC's.

That's what I'm building. The pitch is twelve words. The engine underneath is three systems that move up an autonomy gradient: manual specification, tooled memory, autonomous orchestration.

---

## the engine

The unit is the Holon — a typed note with typed links. Holons organize into holographs. From a holograph you can project anything: documentation, code, schemas, diagrams, a website. Change the artifact, and the change propagates back to the source. Bidirectional by derivation, not by design choice.

The whole thing runs on my laptop in SQLite. Several hundred holons across dozens of holographs. Working protocol server, operational agent orchestration, comprehensive derivation with falsification conditions. The system has been in daily use since early 2026.

---

## the products

Life: Saga makes invisible life systems visible and playable. Six runes — Verve (time and music), Forge (habits as experiments), Lore (sovereign memory), Hearth (domestic life), Trove (wealth), Locus (identity and relationships). Each rune is one dimension of life, made concrete and queryable.

Data lives on your device. No algorithmic feeds, no engagement manipulation, no cloud hostage-taking. Players define their own skills, quests, and progression. Progressive web app — no app store gatekeepers.
