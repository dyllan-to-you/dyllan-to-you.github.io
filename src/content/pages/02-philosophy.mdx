## philosophy

Local-first. Data-sovereign. Community-owned. The solarpunk ethos isn't aesthetic — it's architectural. Every system should make its inhabitants more free, not less.

---

## practice

Recursive specification as design method. Formal modeling before implementation. Convergent systems that derive structure from first principles — assert nothing, derive everything.
